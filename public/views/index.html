What is Cards for Humanity?

As much as we like receiving, we also like to give when possible. Cards for Humanity allows you to donate to a charity of your choice, for extras and upgrades - all while remaining as despicable and awkward as you naturally are.

The game is simple. Each round, one player asks a question from a Black Card, and everyone else answers with their funniest White Card.
